Directions

General Information

Please arrive at least 20 minutes before your scheduled departure time.

Sea Fever is located at Dock N, Liberty Landing Marina, Liberty State Park, Jersey City, NJ. We are about 7 minutes from downtown Manhattan via the Liberty State Park Water Taxi.

Pick-up and return is available at North Cove Marina, NYC for an additional $175.00 for pick-up and $175.00 for return in cash per boat.

Call 201-887-8700 for reservations, schedule, and arrival slip. For transportation info, refer to the sections below.

How to Get to Liberty State Park

By Liberty State Park Water Taxi

Take the Liberty State Park Water Taxi (not the NY Water Taxi or NY Waterway). The water taxi arrives directly at our marina, making it the easiest and fastest way to reach us.

If your charter ends later than the last water taxi run, we will drive you to the nearest PATH station free of charge (Sea Fever Excursions customers only).

Call the water taxi directly at 212-742-1969 for more info. See the water taxi schedule here.

By Subway

North Cove Marina is accessible via the following subway stops:

  • World Trade Center
  • Cortland St.
  • Chambers St.
NYC Subway Map
By Ferry

Take the Liberty State Park Water Taxi from New York’s World Financial Center to Liberty Landing Marina. The fare is $10.00.

By Light Rail

Service is available from Exchange Place every 7–8 minutes starting at 5:30 a.m. daily. Fare is $2.55, and exact change is required if not using the app.

Important Note

Ferry schedules may change or end at certain times. We can arrange free rides to the PATH station for Sea Fever Excursions customers only.
