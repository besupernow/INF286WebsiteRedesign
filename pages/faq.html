What to Bring & FAQ

What to Bring

  • Boat shoes or sneakers to prevent slipping and protect the boat's finish.
  • Camera for capturing memorable moments.
  • Refreshments such as water, juice, soft drinks, and snacks.
  • Sun lotion and sunscreen to protect against sunburn.
  • Layered clothing and a windbreaker for cooler days.
  • Light protective clothing for hotter days.
  • Any special medications you may need. Inform our captain of any medical conditions.
  • Bring only what you need for about 2 hours to avoid overpacking.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of clothing should I wear?

Wear windproof clothing such as a windbreaker. On cooler days, layered clothing is better than a single thick jacket. On hotter days, wear light, natural fiber clothing and a hat with a tie to protect against sun exposure.

What about footwear?

Boat shoes or sneakers are mandatory. They help prevent slipping and protect the deck.

Can I bring snacks or refreshments?

You are welcome to bring snacks and drinks. Alcohol is permitted for those of legal drinking age, with prior approval. We also offer food options, including:

  • Sea Snacks: Soft drinks and bagged snacks (chips, pretzels, etc.).
  • Steamed Shrimp: Fresh steamed shrimp with cocktail sauce, plus the Sea Snack option.
  • Captain's Platter: Homemade wraps from Frankie Bruno's "Mr. Wraps" of Hoboken. Available for "Fill the Boat" guests only.
What items should I secure while onboard?

Keep all valuable items like cameras and sunglasses secured in the cockpit or cabin. The wind and sea often cause items to fall overboard, and they are unlikely to be retrieved.
