Sail the Hudson

Experience the magic of New York City from the water. Our private sailboat charters offer a unique opportunity to see the city's iconic sights from a new perspective. Whether you're looking for a romantic sunset cruise, a family adventure, or a corporate event, we have the perfect boat for you.

Unforgettable Views

Our sailboat charters take you past some of New York's most famous landmarks, including the Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, and the Brooklyn Bridge. You'll also enjoy stunning views of the city skyline and the surrounding waterways. It's an experience you'll never forget.

Personalized Service

At Sea Fever Excursions, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional service to our guests. Our experienced captains and crew will ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable sailing experience. We can accommodate groups of all sizes and can tailor our services to meet your specific needs.

What are we?

We are a private yacht and tour service that works to provide unique and unforgettable sailing experiences in the heart of New York Harbor. Since our establishment, we’ve been committed to offering personalized service, breathtaking views, and a fleet of exceptional vessels.
