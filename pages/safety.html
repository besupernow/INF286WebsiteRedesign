Safety Guidelines

Boat Safety

All our boats are inspected and approved by the US Coast Guard Auxiliary for safety. Our Catalina Yacht 30 is known for its solid build and comfort.

Lifejackets

Inflatable fanny packs and life jackets are available. Children under 13 must wear Coast Guard-approved life jackets while onboard.

Alcohol & Drugs

No drugs or alcohol are permitted without prior approval. Please inform the captain of any life-threatening medical needs or medications.

Motion Sickness

Ginger or motion sickness pills (e.g., Dramamine) can help. Consult your physician before use.

Shoes

Wear boat shoes or sneakers. Leather or hard-soled shoes may slide and damage the boat.

Sun and Wind Exposure

Wear sunblock, polarized sunglasses, and hats with straps. Dress in layers or bring a windbreaker to stay comfortable.

Important Safety Reminder

One hand on the boat, and one hand for yourself. Always hold on!

Always watch for other ships and buoys on the water.
