About Us

Sea Fever Excursions was founded in 2002 with a vision to share the beauty of New York Harbor and the thrill of sailing with everyone. From romantic getaways to corporate outings, we pride ourselves on providing memorable experiences, stunning views, and personalized service. Join us and explore the magic of the Hudson River and beyond.

Our Mission & Values

At Sea Fever Excursions, we aim to create unforgettable sailing experiences while prioritizing safety and environmental stewardship. Our values include:

  • Safety: All boats meet US Coast Guard Auxiliary safety standards.
  • Adventure: Experience New York City like never before.
  • Community: Build lasting memories with friends and family.

Our Fleet

Sea Fever

The Sea Fever is a Catalina Yacht 30, renowned for its solid build and comfortable design. It's the most successful USA-built sailboat ever produced, offering smooth sailing and stunning views.
